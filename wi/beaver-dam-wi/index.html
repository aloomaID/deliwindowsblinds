2. Perform Basic Repairs: For minor issues, such as tangled cords or misaligned slats, you may be able to perform simple repairs yourself. Gently straighten bent slats or adjust the cord to restore functionality. Ensure you follow the manufacturer’s instructions to avoid further damage.
3. Seek Professional Help: If the damage is more severe, such as broken brackets or motorized system malfunctions, it’s best to contact a professional. Deli Windows Blinds offers expert repair services in Beaver Dam Wisconsin ensuring that your blinds are fixed correctly and efficiently.
